Burgers

4 Servings

  • 400g lean beef mince (10% fat)
  • preferably organic 5 spring onions finely chopped
  • 140g finely grated carrot
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp chopped tarragon
  • 1 medium egg beaten
  • 4 wholemeal bread rolls
  • split in half 1½ tsp rapeseed oil
  • 25g watercress
  • 2 large red peppers
  • 100g cherry or baby plum tomatoes
  • halved 2 tsp lime juice 2 tsp snipped chives ¼ very small red onion
  • thinly sliced pinch crushed chillies
